Frequently Asked Questions

How do you best use the site?

DividendData.com is designed as a desktop/laptop web-based research application. It can be use on mobile platforms via the web browser. The best user experience is on larger screens.

Is there a limit on the number of AI chats?

AI usage is set at 100 prompts per month. This is to limit abuse and excess costs. For questions, email support@dividenddata.com

What stocks are in the stock analysis tool?

Access the financials of over 80,000 stocks, ETFs and Mutual Funds. This is across international brokerages. Data in native currency. Highest data quality for NYSE, Nasdaq, and AMEX.

What stocks are available for portfolio tracking?

Dividend Data supports over 80,000 stocks, ETFs, and Funds from exchanges across the globe. Get projected dividend income and up to date prices.

Can you automatically link your brokerage account?

Dividend Data supports automatic linking for US brokerage accounts via services like Plaid and Snaptrade. Included brokerages include, but is not limited to, Robinhood, Merrill, Fidelity, Schwab, Vanguard, Betterment, M1 Finance, Etrade, Interactive Brokers, Betterment, etc.

International Currency Support?

Dividend Data allows you to track in your native currency for portfolio tracking, converting currently to USD, CAD, EURO, GBP, AUD, JPY, etc. Stock Analysis data is presented in the native reported currency for the stock on given exchage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, American Express, Mastercard, and Discover credit/debit cards. Also, you can use Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Cash App Pay. All payments are securely processed by Stripe.

You can cancel your membership at any time. We also have a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the product quality. So no risk in trying it out. For assistance with either please message help or email support@dividenddata.com
